Instructions

1
Click one of the buttons above
This opens the signature in a new browser tab.
2
Highlight everything on the page
Press Ctrl + A to highlight the GIF and link.
3
Copy it
Press Ctrl + C to copy.
4
Open Outlook Signature Settings
Go to Settings → Mail → Signatures → + New signature
(or edit your existing signature)
5
Paste into the signature editor
Click in the editor box and press Ctrl + V
6
Save your signature
Click Save and set it as your default signature.
Note: The animated GIF will play in your emails. When recipients click the "View Vysus Capability Statement" link, they'll be taken to our capability page.
