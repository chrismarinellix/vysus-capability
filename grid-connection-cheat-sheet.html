Grid Connection Conversations

Keep this open during calls - you've got this!

THE FLOW

1. OPEN "Hey! How's things? What are you up to these days?"
2. ASK "Anything interesting in the pipeline?"
3. OFFER "If you need a hand with grid connection stuff, let me know."
4. SEND "I'll send you a quick overview of what we do."

QUESTIONS THAT WORK

  • "What stage is that project at?"
  • "Are you managing the grid connection in-house?"
  • "What's your timeline for connection?"
  • "Who's handling your technical studies?"
  • "Any challenges with the connection process?"

OPENING LINES

  • "Hey [Name], it's been ages! How's things?"
  • "Saw you're at [Company] now - how's that?"
  • "Long time! What are you working on?"
  • "The market's busy - are you seeing the same?"
